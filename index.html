Getting Started

Taking off is really simple. Just download Processing.py and unzip it into a folder of your choice.



Next start, depending on your platform, either processing-py.bat, .sh or .app This will run the default script in workspace/

From there you can either explore more examples in examples.py/, edit the provided default script, or start your own project. The choice is all yours :-)

P.S. You can also drag-drop any Processing.py script on the provided launcher.

About

Jonathan recently gave a talk about Processing to a group of rather bright 8th-graders, as part of a computer-programming summer camp. The kids were really turned on by Processing but they were bogged down by it's C-like syntax.

So a first proof of concept hack was created in a couple of hours, numerous hours later something worth sharing emerged. Today Processing.py is being used successfully in academic, commercial and private scenarios.
